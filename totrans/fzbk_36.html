Part VI: Managing Fuzzing

原文：http://www.fuzzingbook.org/html/06_Managing_Fuzzing.html

This part discusses how to manage fuzzing in the large.

  • Fuzzing in the Large discusses how to create large infrastructures for fuzzing, running millions of tests and managing their results.

  • When to Stop Fuzzing details how to estimate when enough fuzzing is enough – and when you can let your computers work on other tasks.
